## Our Mission

**Section Label:** Our Mission
**Headline:** AI adoption starts at the top.

**Body Text:**
Most AI initiatives fail because they start in the wrong place. They begin with tools, not people. They begin with IT, not leadership. They begin with experiments, not strategy.

Throttl was founded to fix that. We work with management teams — the people who actually run businesses — and give them the knowledge, skills, and support to lead AI adoption from the front.

We do this through two services: **AI Workshops** that certify your leadership team, and **AI Business Transformation** engagements where our team embeds inside your business and does the work with you.

## What We Believe - Our Values

**Section Label:** How We Work
**Headline:** What we believe

### Six Core Values:

1. **Operators, not theorists**
   - We've worked inside businesses. We know what it takes to change how an organisation operates. We don't do slide decks and disappear.

2. **Leadership-first**
   - AI adoption succeeds when it's led from the top. We always start with the management team, not the IT department.

3. **Practical over impressive**
   - We're not here to show you the most advanced AI. We're here to find the AI that actually moves the needle in your business.

4. **Honest about limitations**
   - AI isn't the answer to everything. We'll tell you where it won't help — and focus your energy where it will.

5. **Capability, not dependency**
   - Our goal is for your team to own AI adoption. We build internal capability, not long-term consulting relationships.

6. **Results over activity**
   - We measure success by business outcomes — time saved, decisions improved, revenue generated — not by tools deployed.
