# Throttl AI — Homepage Redesign: Design Concepts

## Context
Two core services to communicate clearly:
1. AI Management Training — train your management team to use AI tools and drive value
2. In-House AI Enablement Teams — Throttl teams work alongside your management to AI-enable your business

**Option A: "The Operator's Briefing"**

- **Design Movement**: Swiss International Typographic Style meets editorial finance/consulting (think Bloomberg, Stripe Press)
- **Core Principles**: Information hierarchy above decoration; every element earns its place; confidence through restraint; black-and-white with a single electric accent
- **Color Philosophy**: Off-white (#F8F7F4) background, near-black (#111) text, one electric accent — a sharp amber (#E8A020). Amber signals urgency and value without the tech-purple cliché.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric editorial grid — large left-anchored headlines, right-side supporting content. Services presented as two large numbered cards side by side.
- **Signature Elements**: Large typographic numerals (01, 02) as section anchors; thin horizontal rules; tight letter-spacing on labels
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Minimal — hover states reveal underlines, CTA button has a subtle fill-sweep animation
- **Animation**: Entrance animations on scroll — headlines slide in from left, stats count up, cards fade up with stagger
- **Typography System**: Display: "Playfair Display" (authoritative serif); Body: "DM Sans" (clean, modern); Labels: uppercase DM Sans 11px tracked wide

**Option B: "Dark Command Center"**

- **Design Movement**: Premium SaaS / dark-mode enterprise (Vercel, Linear, Raycast aesthetic)
- **Core Principles**: Dark-first; high contrast; motion as communication; the two services as the only two things that matter
- **Color Philosophy**: Deep charcoal (#0D0D0D) background, pure white text, electric teal (#00D4AA) as the single accent. Teal signals forward-thinking without being clichéd purple.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed dark hero with a single bold statement; below, two massive service tiles that fill the viewport side-by-side on desktop, stacked on mobile
- **Signature Elements**: Subtle grid dot pattern in hero background; glowing teal borders on service cards; monospace font for labels
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Service cards lift and glow on hover; CTA has a pulsing ring animation
- **Animation**: Hero text reveals character by character; service cards animate in from bottom; smooth scroll with parallax on background grid
- **Typography System**: Display: "Space Grotesk" (geometric, techy); Body: "Inter" (readable); Labels: "JetBrains Mono" (monospace precision)

**Option C: "The Boardroom Brief"**

- **Design Movement**: Corporate Modernism — the aesthetic of McKinsey decks and HBR articles, but with warmth
- **Core Principles**: Clarity as the primary value; two services as the entire story; human over technical; warm neutrals ground the AI subject matter
- **Color Philosophy**: Warm cream (#FAF8F5) background, deep navy (#1A2744) text, a confident coral-orange (#E85D35) accent. Orange breaks from the AI-blue cliché and signals energy and action.
- **Layout Paradigm**: Clean top nav, large hero with a single direct question ("Does your management team know how to use AI?"), then two service blocks presented as a clear choice/path
- **Signature Elements**: Thick left-border accent lines on service cards; clean numbered steps; a simple "two paths" visual metaphor
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Warm, approachable — hover states use gentle background color shifts; CTA is a solid coral button
- **Animation**: Subtle fade-in on scroll; no flashy effects — the content does the work
- **Typography System**: Display: "Fraunces" (warm, distinctive serif); Body: "Outfit" (friendly, modern sans); Labels: uppercase Outfit tracked

</idea>
</response>

---

## Selected Approach
**Option C: "The Boardroom Brief"** — chosen for its clarity, warmth, and direct communication style. The coral accent breaks from AI clichés, and the warm cream palette makes the content feel approachable and trustworthy rather than cold and technical. The layout puts the two services front and center as a clear choice.
